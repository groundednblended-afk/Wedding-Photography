A World of Experience, Focused on You

Hello, I'm Brendan Gibson.

For the past 17 years, my passport has been my portfolio. I have had the privilege to photograph events around the world. driven by my passion for travel and photography and a career that immerses me in a diverse, ever-changing world of visual storytelling.

Despite the world traveling, the moments that stay with me are the ones happening right here at home. My approach to photography and life is rooted in dedication. That is exactly why I built my photography collections to be accessible, straightforward, and bitesize.

Whether you need a quick 30-minute portrait update or want a cinematic video of a surprise proposal, my goal is to bring my experience to your everyday milestones, stress-free.

Our Offerings

From the quiet moment you say "yes" to the grand celebration of your vows, we offer comprehensive photo and video coverage tailored to your unique love story.

  • Traditional Weddings: Full-day coverage, second shooters, and heirloom albums.
  • Elopements & Micro-Weddings: Intimate, adventurous documentation for smaller guest lists.
  • Couples & Engagements: Relaxed, lifestyle portrait sessions to celebrate your connection.
  • Cinematic Videography: Stunning 4K highlight reels to bring your memories to life with motion and sound.
